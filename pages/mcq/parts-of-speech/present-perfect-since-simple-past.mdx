# Tense with "since": Present Perfect + since + Simple Past

**Question:** Many years have passed since his father (die). Which is the correct verb form?

<Tabs items={["a) died", "b) has died", "c) had died", "d) was dying"]}>
  <Tab>
    `died` - **Correct**. The main clause "Many years have passed" is in the
    present perfect tense. According to the rule, the clause following "since"
    must be in the simple past tense.
  </Tab>
  <Tab>
    `has died` - Incorrect. This violates the tense sequence rule with "since".
  </Tab>
  <Tab>
    `had died` - Incorrect. Past perfect would be used if the main clause were
    in the simple past (e.g., "Many years passed since his father had died").
  </Tab>
  <Tab>
    `was dying` - Incorrect. Past continuous implies an ongoing process, but the
    sentence refers to the event of death.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Detailed Explanation ( বিস্তারিত ব্যাখ্যা )

এই বাক্যটিও `since` ব্যবহারের tense sequence নিয়মটি অনুসরণ করে, যা আগের প্রশ্নটিতে আলোচনা করা হয়েছে।

**Grammar Rule for `since`:**

**Rule 1:** `since`-এর আগের clause-টি যদি **Present Tense** (Present Indefinite or Present Perfect) হয়, তবে `since`-এর পরের clause-টি **Simple Past Tense** হবে।

- **Structure:** Present Tense / Present Perfect Tense + `since` + Simple Past (V2)

**Analysis of the sentence:**

- **Main Clause:** "Many years have passed" - এটি Present Perfect Tense-এ আছে (`have passed`)।
- **Conjunction:** since
- **Subordinate Clause:** "his father (die)"

যেহেতু `since`-এর আগের clause-টি Present Perfect Tense-এ আছে, নিয়ম অনুযায়ী `since`-এর পরের clause-টি Simple Past Tense-এ হবে। `die`-এর Simple Past form (V2) হলো `died`।

**Complete Sentence:**

> Many years have passed since his father **died**.
> (তার বাবা মারা যাওয়ার পর অনেক বছর কেটে গেছে।)

<Callout type="info">
  **Learning Hack:** `since`-এর আগের অংশে `is`/`are` বা `has`/`have` দেখলে,
  `since`-এর পরের অংশে verb-এর past form (`V2`) বসিয়ে দিন।
</Callout>

**Example from previous question:**

> It **is** many years since I **came** to Dhaka. (Present Indefinite + since + Simple Past)

**Current question's example:**

> Many years **have passed** since his father **died**. (Present Perfect + since + Simple Past)

উভয় ক্ষেত্রেই `since`-এর পরে Simple Past Tense ব্যবহৃত হয়েছে।

### Why other options are incorrect:

- **b) has died:** Present Perfect। নিয়ম অনুযায়ী এটি ভুল।
- **c) had died:** Past Perfect। এটি ব্যবহৃত হতো যদি প্রথম অংশ Past Tense-এ থাকতো (e.g., "Many years passed...").
- **d) was dying:** Past Continuous। এটি এখানে অর্থপূর্ণ নয়।

**Correct Answer:** a) died
