# Reported Speech: Simple Past/Present Perfect to Past Perfect

**Question:** He said that he (do) the work. Which is the correct verb form?

<Tabs items={["a) did", "b) has done", "c) had done", "d) would do"]}>
  <Tab>
    `did` - Incorrect. This would imply the action happened at the same time as
    the speaking, which is unlikely. The action of doing the work happened
    before he said it.
  </Tab>
  <Tab>
    `has done` - Incorrect. Present perfect cannot be used in a clause following
    a past tense reporting verb (`said`).
  </Tab>
  <Tab>
    `had done` - **Correct**. This follows the rules of backshifting in reported
    speech. Both Simple Past ("I did the work") and Present Perfect ("I have
    done the work") in direct speech change to Past Perfect in indirect speech.
  </Tab>
  <Tab>
    `would do` - Incorrect. This would be used if the direct speech was "I will
    do the work."
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Detailed Explanation ( বিস্তারিত ব্যাখ্যা )

এই বাক্যটি indirect speech (reported speech)-এর একটি ক্লাসিক উদাহরণ। Reporting verb `said` past tense-এ আছে, তাই reported clause-এর verb-কে অবশ্যই backshift করতে হবে।

**Rule of Sequence of Tenses (Backshifting):**
Reporting verb (`said`) past tense-এ থাকলে, direct speech-এর verb form-কে corresponding past tense-এ পরিবর্তন করতে হয়।

Let's analyze the possible direct speech forms:

**Scenario 1: Direct speech is in Simple Past.**

> Direct: He said, "I **did** the work."
> Indirect: He said that he **had done** the work. (Simple Past → Past Perfect)

**Scenario 2: Direct speech is in Present Perfect.**

> Direct: He said, "I **have done** the work."
> Indirect: He said that he **had done** the work. (Present Perfect → Past Perfect)

উভয় ক্ষেত্রেই, indirect speech-এ verb formটি `had done` হয়। কাজটি বলার আগেই সম্পন্ন হয়েছিল, যা past perfect tense (`had done`) দিয়ে সবচেয়ে সঠিকভাবে প্রকাশ করা হয়।

<Callout type="info">
  **Learning Hack:** Reported speech-এ reporting verb past tense-এ থাকলে, direct
  speech-এর Simple Past (`V2`) এবং Present Perfect (`have/has + V3`) উভয়ই Past
  Perfect (`had + V3`)-এ পরিবর্তিত হয়।
</Callout>

### Why other options are incorrect:

- **a) did:** Simple past tense এখানে ভুল, কারণ কাজটি বলার আগেই ঘটেছিল।
- **b) has done:** `said` (past tense) এর সাথে `has done` (present perfect) ব্যবহার করা যায় না।
- **d) would do:** এটি ব্যবহৃত হতো যদি direct speech-এ future intent (`will do`) থাকতো।

**Correct Answer:** c) had done
